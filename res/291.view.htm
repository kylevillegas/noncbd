792<h2>

793<strong>

794The Regenerative Process</strong>

795</h2>

796

797<p>

798In agriculture the regenerative process is defined by a systematic farming practice and principle used to enrich the soil, increase biodiversity, enhance the ecosystem and improves the watershed. This practice has been part of the agricultural industry for many years. Most of the approaches used with this practice were replaced by commercial farmers who preferred larger yields. The idea is not to sustain the farm when it is failing. That is not the best way to approach this.</p>

799

800<h2>

801<strong>

802Holistic Approach</strong>

803</h2>

804

805<p>

806For an organic and holistic approach to farming, the regenerative marijuana process is best because it produces marijuana that is bio-diverse. In other words, compassion for the marijuana plant is the ultimate goal as it is in wine vineyards. The marijuana strain grown in this environment produces a top-tiered yield with unique traits. The regenerative farming practices for marijuana is more than being sustainable. It is about putting life into your garden and then your crop. It means maintaining a higher quality agricultural system. Let us take a look at the core principles of this practice.</p>

807

808<h2>

809<strong>

810The Concept</strong>

811</h2>

812

813<p>

814Central to regenerative farming is the concept of conscious cultivation. What is this and what does it involved? It involves providing plants with not just want things that are immediately needed, but what is potential necessary. As a farmer, you have to see the <a href="https://cannabistraininguniversity.com/blog/growing-marijuana/marijuana-plant-growth-cycle-stages/">

815marijuana plant</a>

816 as more than part of your crop. With regenerative marijuana farming, you get to see the biological and eco-systemic fit of the plant.</p>

817

818<h2>

819<strong>

820Seed to Seed</strong>

821</h2>

822

823<p>

824When you administer the regenerative practice it will involve a seed to seed process. What does this mean? It means that you are observing the whole life cycle of your marijuana plant. You are also creating your own <a rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://www.royalseedbank.com/how-to-buy-marijuana-seeds/" target="_blank">

825cannabis seeds.</a>

826 There are some marijuana farmers who might consider using clones, but the ultimate goal, whether you use the regenerative practice or clones, is to create unique marijuana genetics. You develop a relationship with your plant by working on it yourself in a distinct environment; something you could not have achieved if you were growing it in a different climate or if you preferred to have someone else do it all for you.</p>
